Description

The traditional recipe for lasagna, just like your mother used to make in Italy.

Ingredients

  • Water
  • Flour
  • Tomato
  • Minced meat
  • Herbs
  • Mozerella cheese
  • Basilicum

Steps

  1. Start by mixing the water and the flour to a dough
  2. Roll out the dough into thin slices
  3. Cut the dough into rectengular pieces of the same size
  4. In the mean time also start preparing the sauce
